üß† Micronutrients (athlete checklist)
  • Iron (oxygen transport): red meat, ikan bilis, leafy greens, legumes; pair with Vitamin C.
  • Calcium + Vitamin D (bone & muscle): dairy/tofu/small fish with bones; sunlight/fortified foods.
  • B Vitamins (energy metabolism): whole grains, meats, eggs, leafy greens.
  • Magnesium + Zinc (repair/immunity): nuts/seeds, seafood, meat, legumes.
  • Antioxidants (Vitamins C & E): fruits/veg; nuts/seeds.
